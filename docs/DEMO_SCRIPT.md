## ðŸ“ Q&A Preparation

### Likely Questions

**Q: "What's the performance overhead of encryption?"**
> A: "About 50-100ms for encryption/decryption per query. The larger cost is LLM inference itself - 1-2 seconds. Encryption is typically <5% of total latency."

**Q: "Can you scale this to thousands of users?"**
> A: "Yes. The frontend is stateless and deploys to CDN (Vercel). The backend is containerized and horizontally scalable. Nillion's nilAI is designed for production scale with load balancing."

**Q: "What about model accuracy with encrypted data?"**
> A: "The model operates on decrypted data inside the TEE - there's no accuracy loss. The encryption protects data in transit and at rest, but inference happens on plaintext within the secure enclave."

**Q: "Is this actually HIPAA compliant or just 'HIPAA-ready'?"**
> A: "The architecture satisfies HIPAA's technical safeguards: encryption, access controls, audit logging, integrity controls. For production compliance, you'd need Business Associate Agreements, additional policy work, and compliance audits - but the technical foundation is compliant."

**Q: "What happens if the TEE is compromised?"**
> A: "TEEs are hardware-based security - compromising them requires physical access to the chip and sophisticated attacks. But even if that happened, the attacker would only access data being processed at that moment - historical data is encrypted at rest. Plus, attestation would fail, alerting us immediately."

**Q: "Can we use this with our existing medical models?"**
> A: "Yes. The architecture is model-agnostic. You'd need to deploy your model to nilAI (which supports most Hugging Face models) and adjust the system prompt. The encryption and TEE infrastructure stays the same."

**Q: "What's the cost?"**
> A: "Frontend: $0 on Vercel free tier. Backend: ~$20-50/month for basic VM. Nillion nilAI: Contact Nillion for pricing - it's based on compute usage similar to AWS pricing."
